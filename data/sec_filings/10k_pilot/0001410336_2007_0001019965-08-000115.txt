Other necessary controls are in place to minimize the <br>risk of such errors.</DIV>
<DIV style="position:absolute;top:821;left:152">With respect to the specific pool assets and asset-backed securities related to the Investor Reporting Errors, the errors <br>did not have, and are not reasonable likely in the future to have, any material impact or effect on pool asset <br>performance, servicing of the pool assets and payments or expected payments on the mortgage-backed securities.<br> <br>In this transaction, LaSalle served as custodian.
