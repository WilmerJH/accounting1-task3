      This Annual Report on Form 10-K (the "Report") is filed with respect to
the trust entitled CWABS Asset-Backed Certificates Trust 2006-9 (the "Issuing
Entity") formed pursuant to the pooling and servicing agreement dated as of
June 1, 2006 (the "Pooling and Servicing Agreement") among CWABS, Inc., as
depositor (the "Company"), Countrywide Home Loans, Inc., as a seller (a
"Seller"), Park Sienna LLC, as a seller (a "Seller"), Park Monaco Inc., as a
seller (a "Seller"), Countrywide Home Loans Servicing LP, as master servicer
(the "Master Servicer"), and The Bank of New York, as trustee (the "Trustee"),
for the issuance of Asset-Backed Certificates, Series 2006-9 (the
"Certificates").
